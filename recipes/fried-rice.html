Easy Fried Rice

Quick and easy fried rice with chicken, vegetables, and soy sauce. Great for a quick weeknight meal.

A quick fried rice recipe like you get at your favorite Chinese restaurant.
Leftover rice, plus a couple of eggs, baby carrots, peas, and soy sauce is all you need.
Garnish with sliced green onions, if desired.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups cooked rice
  • 1 tablespoon vegetable oil
  • 1 cup chopped cooked chicken
  • 1 cup chopped carrots
  • 1 cup frozen green peas
  • 2 large eggs, beaten
  • 2 tablespoons soy sauce to taste
  • 2 teaspoon sesame oil to taste

Steps

  1. Place carrots in a small saucepan and cover with water. Bring to a low boil and cook for 3 to 5 minutes.
  2. Stir in peas, then immediately drain in a colander.
  3. Heat a wok over high heat. Pour in vegetable oil, then stir in carrots, peas, and garlic; cook for about 30 seconds.
  4. Add eggs; stir quickly to scramble eggs with vegetables.
  5. Stir in cooked rice. Add soy sauce and toss rice to coat. Drizzle with sesame oil and toss again.
